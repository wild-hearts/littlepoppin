Refunds & Returns

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Your rights under Australian Consumer Law

Our products come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and to compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if they fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.

If an item arrives faulty, damaged, or not as described, please contact us and we’ll make it right with a repair, replacement, or refund as appropriate.

Change of mind

If you simply change your mind, we’re happy to accept a return within 30 days of delivery, provided the swaddle is unused, unwashed, and in its original condition and packaging. Change-of-mind return postage is at your cost.

How to request a return or refund

  1. Contact us on 0456 367 013 or via our contact page with your order details.
  2. We’ll confirm the return address and next steps.
  3. Once we’ve received and inspected the item, approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Stripe.

Timing

Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days of approval. Your bank or card provider may take additional time to show the funds.

Hygiene note

For health and hygiene reasons, items that have been washed or used cannot be returned for change of mind, but your consumer guarantee rights for faulty goods always apply.
