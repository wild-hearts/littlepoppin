# Little Poppin — Collection 01 (first 10 designs) — DREAMY NURSERY WALL ART

Ready-to-paste Midjourney prompts, built from POD_DESIGN_PLAYBOOK.md. Soft, dreamy, whimsical
storybook — framed as calming nursery wall art (above-the-crib decor). All `--ar 4:5` (fits
8×10 / 16×20). Gallery sets: animals (3,4,8) · celestial & whimsy (5,6,7) · floral/botanical (1,2,9,10).
Tip: push `--stylize` to 600–700 for even dreamier. Large prints need an external upscale to 300 DPI.
