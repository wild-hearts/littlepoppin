Our Story

Made with Love in Canberra

You did it — you found the perfect baby swaddle. Your search is finally over.

Little Poppin is a Canberra-based baby swaddle brand created by a mother who wanted something truly special for her little one. We believe every baby deserves the softest, most breathable wrap — and every parent deserves to feel proud of what they drape over their little bundle of joy.

Our swaddles are crafted from a premium 70% Bamboo / 30% Cotton blend — softer than cotton, naturally breathable, and beautifully printed with elegant designs unlike anything else on the market.

And the magic doesn't stop at the cot: our art print collections bring the same enchanted wonder to nursery walls and little bedrooms — dreamy watercolour worlds, delivered as instant downloads.

A portion of every purchase goes towards a children's charity making a real difference for disadvantaged children around the world.

Why Little Poppin

Everything Your Baby Deserves

Ultra Soft & Luxurious

Our 70% bamboo / 30% cotton blend is softer than cotton with a luxurious feel. Prewashed and gets even softer with every wash.

Breathable & Temperature Regulating

Open muslin weave fabric allows natural body temperature regulation, moisture absorption and better airflow — a safer, more comfortable sleep for baby.

Generous 47 × 47 Inch Size

Much larger than traditional swaddles — making it easier to wrap baby snugly and possible to swaddle comfortably for longer as your baby grows.

Unique Elegant Designs

Our prints stand apart from run-of-the-mill cartoon patterns. Vibrant, artistic and stylish — beautiful enough to show off when you take your baby out.

Multi-Purpose Essential

So much more than a swaddle. Use it as a nursing cover, change mat, burp cloth, stroller cover, tummy time blanket and more — it does it all.

Give Back to Children

With every purchase, a portion of proceeds is donated to a children's charity supporting disadvantaged kids around the world. Buy beautifully, give meaningfully.
