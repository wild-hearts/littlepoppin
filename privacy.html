Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Little Poppin (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy and handles personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

What we collect

When you place an order we collect the details needed to fulfil it: your name, shipping address, email address and phone number. This information is entered directly into Stripe Checkout, our payment processor.

Payment information

Card payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not see or store your full card number — Stripe handles card data under PCI-DSS standards. See Stripe’s privacy policy.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (for example, pages viewed). This may involve cookies and aggregated usage data. See Google’s privacy policy.

How we use your information

  • To process and ship your order and contact you about it
  • To respond to enquiries you send us
  • To meet our legal and tax obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

Storage & third parties

Your information is handled by the services we use to run the store, including Stripe (payments), our hosting provider Vercel, and (if enabled) our email provider for order notifications. Each maintains its own security and privacy practices.

Access, correction & contact

You can ask us to access or correct the personal information we hold about you. Little Poppin is a brand of Wild Hearts Publishing Pty Ltd (ABN 34 695 911 025). Contact us at info@wildheartspublishing.com.au, on 0456 367 013, or via our contact page.
